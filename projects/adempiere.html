Description

Fedena School ERP System provides user-friendly dashboards with login access for teachers, non-teaching staff, students, parents and management personnel of your institution. The various modules available in Fedena facilitate all the processes of your institution, from admission of new students to generating transfer certificates when students complete their studies.
